“It would cost the taxpayer more to cancel HS2 than to follow through”

In the event HS2 were cancelled, the money for it would disappear. It's like a loan or a mortgage, you can only use it for what you've said you'll use it for.

All costs following a hypothetical decision to cancel HS2 (early contract terminations, restoration of land, etc) would be payed out of what's called "Operational Expenditure" (or "OpEx"), which comes from your taxes.
