Welcome to Zune

Chances are, your existing android app will port painlessly to Zune, as zOS is based on Android, and has a compatibility system for Play Services. This enables zOS to give users and developers everything they'd normally expect from Android, but without the privacy nightmare of Play Services or Google.

One more thing

If your app has over 1000 downloads on iOS or Google Play, you can publish it FREE on the Zune Store!
